In this webpage experience, we shall journey through the esteemed tenor saxophone player John Coltrane's album dubbed "Lush Life."

Below you will find a photo of the album cover for it's original release in May of 1957.
Further below you will find a video containing the final tune on the album: "I hear a Rhapsody"
